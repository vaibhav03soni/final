NEED HELP?

Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, therapy, and mental well-being.

How do I book a therapy session?
You can book a therapy session by selecting a therapist on our platform and choosing a suitable time slot.
Are online sessions available?
Yes, we offer both online and in-person sessions depending on your preference and availability.
What’s the difference between therapy and coaching?
Therapy focuses on mental health and emotional healing, while coaching helps you set and achieve personal goals.
Do I need a subscription to access services?
No, you can book individual sessions without a subscription. Subscription plans offer discounts and flexibility.
Can I switch therapists if I don’t feel the right connection?
Absolutely. We understand the importance of the right match and make it easy for you to switch therapists anytime.
Is my information and session history kept confidential?
Yes, your privacy is our priority. All session records are securely stored and kept confidential.
